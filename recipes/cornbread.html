Best Ever Cornbread

This cornbread is so good it is insane!

If you follow the steps below, you will soon be eating the best cornbread you have ever eaten!

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
